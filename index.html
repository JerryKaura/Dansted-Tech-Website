About Dansted Tech

Networking Services
Dansted tech is a dream idea of high school students from dandora secondary school. Their idea was to use science and technology to dive change in their hood. Dansted tech began operations in dandora in 2009. Since then dansted as continued to venture in IT related fields. Today dansted is offering internet services, computer maintenance and sales,networking services and security systems in variousparts of Kenya. Through Dansted tech over 100 youths have benefit with skills and knowledge transfers. “WE ARE DRIVEN BY YOUR NEEDS”
